I am an Assistant Professor of Philosophy at the Copernicus Center for Interdisciplinary Studies of the Jagiellonian University. Before that, I completed my PhD (December 2020) as a member of LOGOS research group in analytic philosophy at the University of Barcelona. During my PhD I was a visiting student at Rutgers and the University of St Andrews (Arch√© research group).

I work at the intersection of the philosophy of mind with other sub-disciplines of philosophy: philosophy of language, social epistemology, philosophy of psychology & psychiatry.

My current research focuses mainly on the philosophical underpinnings of psychotherapy.
